Client's Needs

Naillabo Owner

I want to have a booking system, they can easily book the services they want, and their booking information will be sent to my email.

Get 15 New customers from Scratch

I set up an online booking system for the client using Tally Forms, chosen for its free unlimited forms, responses, data security, and quick deployment. The system allows customers to select services and appointment times. Upon submission, customers receive an email confirmation and are directed to a thank you page. To date, the system has successfully facilitated 15 new customers.

Naillabo Owner

I don't have so much time to take care of the website, could u help me to build an automated response robot?

AI Robot Customer Service

Naillabo Salon's website features an AI chatbot that assists customers 24/7. It answers questions, helps with bookings, and suggests services anytime based on given material. This convenient feature not only improves customer service but also significantly reduces the cost of staff time spent on responding to routine inquiries.

Naillabo Owner

SEO Optimization

After SEO optimization, Naillabo Salon's website showed great results. It appeared 7.65k times in search results, meaning 7.65k potential customers saw the salon's information. Out of these, 212 people clicked on the website link to learn more about the salon's services. The website's average position in search results was 18.4. This makes it easier for more people to find Naillabo Salon online.

Touch target and slider layout optimization

  • Sufficient spacing in navigation ensures comfortable finger tapping and aligns with mobile design best practices
  • Changed from horizontal to vertical slider layout, preventing content overlap and providing cleaner separation between images and text

SEO Strategy

Location-Focused Keywords Boost Salon Visibility

I focused the SEO strategy on local searches to attract nearby customers more likely to book with Naillabo Salon. This involved combining nail salon services with "near me" keywords and using location-specific terms like "Richmond" and "Vancouver". The approach positioned Naillabo Salon in searches from people nearby looking for specific services, increasing booking chances.

Long-Tail Keywords for Targeted Traffic

I also selected long-tail keywords related to specific salon services. These had lower search volumes but were highly targeted and easier to optimize for.

Service-Specific Keywords

I optimized the website's SEO by strategically placing keywords and creating separate sections for each main service. Using professional SEO tools like Semrush, I improved the overall SEO performance while maintaining readability, originality, and an appropriate tone of voice. These enhancements aim to boost our search engine rankings and attract more potential clients searching for nail and lash services in our area.

Backlink Acquisition

I focused on acquiring backlinks from diverse channels to improve the website's authority and search rankings.

Google Maps Reviews

I focused on acquiring Google Maps reviews to improve the website's local search visibility and customer trust.

Takeaways

H1 Tags

H1 tags are crucial for SEO as they help search engines understand the structure and content of your website.One page should only have one H1 tag. Don't try to cover everything in one page.

Accessibility

Color contrast and button spacing are important for accessibility. I used color contrast checker to ensure the website is accessible for people with visual impairments.

Design Mobile First

Mobile search for a local business is more likely than desktop search. So I would consider to design the website for the mobile version and expand to desktop version.
