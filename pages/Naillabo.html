    SEO Strategy

    The website has relatively low traffic, so our first step is to aim for more visitors.I use google search console and semrush to analyze the website's SEO performance, and get the following insights:

    Focus on Local SEO

    Many high-search-volume keywords are associated with local businesses, making it worthwhile to focus on local SEO. Achieving higher rankings for local business terms can drive more customers to book through the website.

    Target Strategic Keywords

    Identify and optimize competitors' keywords as well as existing keywords with impressions that have high search volumes, low competition, and no local business listings shown.

    Expand Service Content

    Service-related keywords are not fully covered yet, so it's better to add more pages to target these keywords. Start by displaying relevant content, then focus on optimizing keyword rankings.

    Link Building

    Find backlinks from other nail salon websites or places where we can submit our link. This will help build trust and authority in search results, improve our ranking, and attract more users to our site.

    Iteration

    Home Page & Header Optimization

    Many mobile users scrolled down, but only a small percentage reached the bottom. I shorten the page length and turn the testimonials and services into a carousel. Additionally, making the header sticky so users don't have to scroll down and then back up again.

    Contact Page & Footer Optimization

    I removed the 'contact page' and anchored the contact information in the footer instead, along with adding our key advantage from research— 'free parking available.' This simplifies the website structure while keeping essential details visible on every page.

    Gallery Page Optimization

    12.5% of people are more interested in the gallery's categories, indicating a strong interest in images. Therefore, I increased the emphasis on image displays.

    Additionally, 17.85% of users click the Instagram button instead of viewing the images directly. To address this, I integrated Instagram's API to automatically update images and videos, saving time on future maintenance.

    Service Page Optimization

    According to the SEO analysis, the main related service keywords, such as 'manicure,' 'pedicure,' and 'eyelash,' are not covered. Instead of cramming all services onto the homepage, I created separate pages for each service to optimize the service-focused keywords and improve the website's visibility.

    Pricing Page Added

    Based on negative reviews on Google Maps, many customers complained about the lack of price transparency. To address this, I convinced the owner to introduce basic service prices and added a pricing page, which was initially resisted.

    Style Guide

    Takeaways

    Design Foundations

    Define the style guide, responsive breakpoints, and reusable components first, as they will help maintain consistency and improve efficiency when designing and building the website.

    Problem Solving

    Some technical or complex requirements can be solved by online tutorials or some tools or with the help of AI, just be patient and you will find the solution.

    Design Mobile First

    Mobile search for a local business is more likely than desktop search. So I would consider to design the website for the mobile version and expand to desktop version.
