Outcome

I completed a user flow for joining an event through sportpal, analyzed the entire process for potential users, and standardized the app's style guide. Finally, I created a prototype and test on the Maze platform, identified three issues, and made improvements.

User Test-Maze

The purpose of this test is to find out what problem they will meet when going through the app to join a sports event.

Basically, it is easy for them to go through the mission but there are also some areas for improvement.

Through the heatmap, we can see people find it hard to find the sports events at the beginning. But they learn how to get it for another try.The misclick rate is 64%-27%-18%.

From the heatmap, I found that users tend to click the most obvious place, where the competition function remains. So I just change to the most obvious place link to the sports events since that is my main function. And the competition page I just add an icon to the sport selection page if people want to announce the sport as a competition.

This task is to let people join the basketball events from the beginning to the end.Users join the events through different flows. So it shows 3 people navigating outside the expected path.

However,there are two ways to go to join the basketball event. One is to join basketball events through the overall sport page. The other is to select the basketball category page to join the basketball event. Two options are both to get to the final page. So people mistaking to join the events through category is acceptable.

Since people mistaking to join the events through category is acceptable, I don’t need to change anything.

When users are asked to join the basketball through the basketball category, 36% users do not directly choose the category.The category is not obvious enough.

Besides,there is one suggestion for the category order. It’s much more logical to make it in alphabetical order.

I made a slight change.Cut half of the sports icon, make it more obvious for users to slide the icon to choose the category.
