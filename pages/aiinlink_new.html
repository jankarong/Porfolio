Overview

Problem

AI tools are overwhelming and lack clear community support, making it difficult for users to learn and discuss.

Introduction

AIinlink is a AI tool community that helps users discover, learn, and share AI tools. The platform features curated AI tools, simple tutorials, and a community-driven approach to make AI more accessible to everyday users.
