Introduction

NailLabo Salon's website features online booking, service showcases, and an AI chatbot. The project integrated SEO optimization to enhance online visibility. This user-friendly platform effectively presents the salon's services while improving search engine rankings, aiming to attract and engage potential clients through improved digital presence.

Nail Salon Booking system : Get 8 Appointments by far

I set up an online booking system for the client using Tally forms. The system allows customers to select services and appointment times. Upon submission, customers receive an email confirmation and are directed to a thank you page. To date, the system has successfully facilitated 8 online bookings.

AI Robot Customer Service

Naillabo Salon's website features an AI chatbot that assists customers 24/7. It answers questions, helps with bookings, and suggests services anytime. This convenient feature not only improves customer service but also significantly reduces the cost of staff time spent on responding to routine inquiries.

SEO Optimization

After SEO optimization, Naillabo Salon's website showed great results. It appeared 1,060 times in search results, meaning 1,060 potential customers saw the salon's information. Out of these, 114 people clicked on the website link to learn more about the salon's services. The website's average position in search results was 14.9. This makes it easier for more people to find Naillabo Salon online.

Outcome

The NailLabo website achieved 1.06k impressions, 114 clicks, and 8 bookings. It successfully implements online appointments, a photo gallery, customer reviews, and social media links. These features have effectively improved the salon's digital presence and customer engagement.

SEO-Optimized

I focused the SEO strategy on local searches to attract nearby customers more likely to book with Naillabo Salon. This involved combining nail salon services with "near me" keywords and using location-specific terms like "Richmond" and "Vancouver". The approach positioned Naillabo Salon in searches from people nearby looking for specific services, increasing booking chances.

I also selected long-tail keywords related to specific salon services. These had lower search volumes but were highly targeted and easier to optimize for.

I optimized the website's SEO by strategically placing keywords and creating separate sections for each main service. Using professional SEO tools like Semrush, I improved the overall SEO performance while maintaining readability, originality, and an appropriate tone of voice. These enhancements aim to boost our search engine rankings and attract more potential clients searching for nail and lash services in our area.

Challenging

Even after implementing SEO strategies, the improvement in website rankings has been limited. Skills in acquiring backlinks from diverse channels need improvement.

Next Steps

Incorporate the most effective keywords into the homepage to improve search rankings. Additionally, utilize social media platforms and Google Maps to increase backlinks, thereby enhancing the site's overall SEO performance.

Takeaway
