Client's Needs

"I want to have a booking system, they can easily book the services they want, and their booking information will be sent to my email." --- NailLabo Salon Owner

Securing 8 New Appointments from Scratch

I set up an online booking system for the client using Tally Forms, chosen for its free unlimited forms, responses, data security, and quick deployment. The system allows customers to select services and appointment times. Upon submission, customers receive an email confirmation and are directed to a thank you page. To date, the system has successfully facilitated 8 online bookings

"I don't have so much time to take care of the website, could u help me to build an automated response robot?" --- NailLabo Salon Owner

AI Robot Customer Service

Naillabo Salon's website features an AI chatbot that assists customers 24/7. It answers questions, helps with bookings, and suggests services anytime based on given material. This convenient feature not only improves customer service but also significantly reduces the cost of staff time spent on responding to routine inquiries.

"I want when customers search the keyword, my salon website shows at the front page." --- NailLabo Salon Owner

SEO Optimization

After SEO optimization, Naillabo Salon's website showed great results. It appeared 1,060 times in search results, meaning 1,060 potential customers saw the salon's information. Out of these, 114 people clicked on the website link to learn more about the salon's services. The website's average position in search results was 14.9. This makes it easier for more people to find Naillabo Salon online.

SEO Strategy

1.Location-Focused Keywords Boost Salon Visibility

I focused the SEO strategy on local searches to attract nearby customers more likely to book with Naillabo Salon. This involved combining nail salon services with "near me" keywords and using location-specific terms like "Richmond" and "Vancouver". The approach positioned Naillabo Salon in searches from people nearby looking for specific services, increasing booking chances.

2.Long-Tail Keywords for Targeted Traffic

I also selected long-tail keywords related to specific salon services. These had lower search volumes but were highly targeted and easier to optimize for.

    Takeaways

    Video Observation

    We observed users' behavior and interaction patterns during the testing phase.

    1

    Ineffective CTAs

    Users had hard times locating CTAs to order a drink or download the app, which are the most important features on the site.

    2

    Hard-to-Find Banners

    Users struggled to locate the "Golden Hour" drinks banner and other promotional content, indicating ineffective design, that hides affordance.

    3

    Confusing Layout

    The website is poorly optimized for desktop use with a hamburger menu and broken card layout when browsing for drinks.

    SUS Calculation

    We created the SUS questionnaire in Google Sheets.

    Chatime's website scored 61 on the SUS scale, below the 68-point average. With scores ranging from 47.5 to 72.5, this indicates clear usability issues in navigation and feature discovery that need improvement.
