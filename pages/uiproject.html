Soulflow

This project is a meditation app concept offering guided sessions for relaxation, mindfulness, and sleep improvement. It features a range of practices from 1-minute to 15-minute durations, suitable for beginners to advanced users. The app includes focused breathing, anxiety relief, and family-oriented meditations. It offers a free month trial, then charges $5.99 monthly, aiming to make meditation accessible to a diverse user base.
