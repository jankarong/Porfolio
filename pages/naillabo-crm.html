NailLabo CRM System

Watch the comprehensive demo of our custom-built CRM solution that transformed salon operations

Overview

Problem

The salon was using multiple tools for different tasks, leading to inefficient workflows and increased costs. They needed a centralized system to manage bookings, customer data, and business operations.

Solution

I designed and developed a custom CRM system that seamlessly integrates booking management, customer database, staff scheduling, salary and income calculation, and automated workflows. Tailored specifically for small businesses, the system ensures cost-effectiveness and operational efficiency.
