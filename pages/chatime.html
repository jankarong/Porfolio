    Introduction

    "Chatime's website catches the eye, but its user experience needs improvement. Our focus areas:

  • Increasing app downloads
  • Boosting promotional drink sales
  • Enhancing user experience

    • Through user behavior analysis and testing, we recommend:

  • Optimizing promotional banners
  • Streamlining the ordering process
  • Redesigning the drink menu layout

    • Hypotheses

    Hypothesis 1

    We believe that a 25% increase in drink orders will be achieved if new customers successfully identify consistent call-to-action buttons.

    Hypothesis 2

    We believe that a 15% increase in specialty/seasonal drink sales will be accomplished if users follow a more direct purchasing flow.

    Hypothesis 3

    We believe that implementing a more effective banner will introduce users to app-exclusive deals and promotions which leads to a 20% increase in app downloads.

    Screener Questions

    Our screening process focused on three key areas:

  • Drink preferences
  • Online ordering habits
  • Promotional engagement

    Rainbow Sheets

    Video Observation

    We observed users' behavior and interaction patterns during the testing phase.

    1

    Ineffective CTAs

    Users had hard times locating CTAs to order a drink or download the app, which are the most important features on the site.

    2

    Hard-to-Find Banners

    Users struggled to locate the "Golden Hour" drinks banner and other promotional content, indicating ineffective design, that hides affordance.

    3

    Confusing Layout

    The website is poorly optimized for desktop use with a hamburger menu and broken card layout when browsing for drinks.

    SUS Calculation

    We created the SUS questionnaire in Google Sheets.

    Chatime's website scored 61 on the SUS scale, below the 68-point average. With scores ranging from 47.5 to 72.5, this indicates clear usability issues in navigation and feature discovery that need improvement.

    Recommendations

    UI Elements

    • Redesign banner interaction.
    • Redesign and improve visibility of CTAs.
    • Optimize the 'drinks' page for desktop, utilizing card layout.
    • Replace hamburger menu with navigational bar for desktop.

    Other

    • Optimize images to decrease loading time.
    • Connect 'Our Drink' page to the ordering page.
    • Dedicate a separate section for the App of the home page
