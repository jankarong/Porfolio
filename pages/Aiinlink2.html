Overview

Problem

Many people find it challenging to navigate the vast landscape of AI tools and struggle to effectively integrate them into their daily lives due to the lack of a centralized, user-friendly platform.

Introduction

AIinlink is a WordPress-based community platform that helps users discover, learn, and share AI tools. The platform features curated AI tools, simple tutorials, and a community-driven approach to make AI more accessible to everyday users.

    • Target Audience

    Instead of high-level users, we will focus on beginners and intermediate users.

    Competitive Analysis

    We found these pain points for competitors

    • Overwhelming AI tool
    • Don’t know how to use the tool
    • Do not have a community to comment or interact

    So we still have opportunities

    • Build a community where users can learn simple tutorials and share AI tips together
    • Focus on curated AI tool recommendations rather than mixed collections
    • Fast launch of quality content targeting easy-to-rank keywords can boost SEO

    Business Model

    Business Goals

    • Get first 50 sign up users
    • Get first paying customer

    Product Goals

    • Get more users to find AI tools on the website
    • Get more users to submit AI tools
    • Increase paid users while maintaining free user base
    • Improve registration conversion

    Design Goals

    Due to the time constraint, I only focus on the MVP for this project. However, I will iterate the design base on the feedback from the users and the product goals.

    Enhance AI Tool Discovery and Accessibility

    • Optimize search and categorization to help users find AI tools
    • Design an intuitive tool showcase interface highlighting core functions and use cases
    • Implement smart recommendation modules on homepage

    Optimize AI Tool Destail Page Experience

    • Design a streamlined tutorial flow enabling users to complete their simple AI tool tutorial
    • Provide interactive tutorials for hands-on learning
    • Implement clear visual cues and help information at critical steps

    Streamline AI Tool Submission Process

    • Simplify tool submission,delete unnecessary information
    • Provide clear submission guidelines and best practice examples
    • Design a visual feedback after users sumbit the tool.

    Optimize Paid Conversion Experience

    • Design clear feature comparison between free and paid versions
    • Strategically showcase upgrade benefits after users experience free version value
    • Offer flexible payment plans to meet diverse user needs

    Improve Registration Flow Conversion

    • Streamline registration process to 3 steps or fewer
    • Support one-click login via major platforms
    • Design progressive information collection to avoid overwhelming users
