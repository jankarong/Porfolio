    Here are some of the key findings:

    Not Beginner-Friendly

    • Lack of Beginner-Friendliness (8/8)

      Tools or interfaces are not intuitive or easy for beginners to use.

    • Missing Written Guides (8/8)

      No clear step-by-step tutorials or examples to help users apply the tools in their daily life.

    • Missing Video Tutorials (5/8)

      No simple video tutorials or use case examples to show how the tools can be used effectively.

    • Unclear Feature Descriptions(4/8)

      No simple video tutorials or use case examples to show how the tools can be used effectively.

    No Simplified Tool Selection

    • No Personalized Tool Recommendations (3/8)

      The platform does not provide tailored tool suggestions for specific tasks or needs.

    • Lack of Tool Comparisons (7/8)

      No clear comparisons of tools (e.g., pros/cons, pricing, or features) to help decision-making.

    • Unclear Free and Cost-Effective Options（3/8）

      Difficulty identifying which AI tools are completely free or provide the best value for money.

    • No Solution for Choice Overload （5/8）

      The platform overwhelms users with too many options with poor filters or curated guidance.

    Poor Community and Support

    • Lack of Community Engagement and Interaction （5/8）

      No active community for discussions or feedback, and users lack interactive features such as liking, favoriting, commenting, or viewing content popularity.

    • No Regular Updates or Support （6/8）

      The platform lacks consistent updates and fresh content on tool trends, with unclear frequency or identification of new additions.

    Product Goals

    Based on user needs and competitive analysis, I plan to design AIinlink in three phases:

    First Phase: Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

    AI Tool Selection page

    • Categories like design, image toola, and Planning for intuitive navigation.
    • Basic search functionality to help users find tools quickly.

    AI Tool Detail Page

    • Tool descriptions, use cases, and a simple tutorial (video + text).
    • Focus on clarity and ease of understanding for beginner users.

    Basic AI Community

    • Allow users to upload AI tools they've discovered.
    • Provide discussion spaces for questions and sharing experiences.

    Login, Sign-Up & Pricing Page

    • Users can create an account to save favorites, upload tools, and interact in discussions.
    • Browsing tools and viewing details are allowed without logging in.
    • Personalized Recommendations & Advanced Discovery
      • Use AI to suggest tools based on preferences and history.
      • Expand filter options (task type, pricing, popularity, ratings).
      • Add side-by-side tool comparison with metrics like pros/cons and reviews.
    • Enhanced Community Engagement
      • Introduce user-generated content (blogs, use cases) with likes, views, and comments.
      • Improve discovery with search, filters, and sorting (recent, trending, popular).
    • Improved User Experience & Monetization
      • Offer guided onboarding and dashboards for tracking favorites.
      • Launch a freemium model with basic features free and premium features (analytics, filters, ad-free) as subscription-based.
    • Premium Subscription Model
      • Provide access to premium features like advanced recommendations, in-depth tool analysis, and early access to new tools.
      • Add functionality to export curated tool lists for professional or business use.
    • Advertising and Partner Integration
      • Offer non-intrusive advertising options for AI tool providers based on user preferences.
      • Partner with leading AI developers to offer exclusive content or discounts to platform users.
    • Community and Third-Party Integrations
      • Introduce gamification elements like badges, rewards, and leaderboards to encourage community participation.
      • Launch a mentoring program where advanced users guide beginners in exchange for rewards.
      • Enable API integrations for businesses to embed AI Inlink recommendations and collaborate with tool providers for seamless workflow integrations.

    Iteration

    Because users spend most of their time on the community page, and a strong community boosts growth and sets us apart from competitors, I chose to optimize the AI community page first.

    As posts continue to grow, users need enhanced filter and search functionalities, along with the ability to save favorite and like relevant content. They also want a more intuitive and clear way to view posts.

    Based on user needs, available resources, and the platform's early growth and analysis of other community websites, we have determined the necessary feature in Phase two.
